Meshuggah Tribute Page

Bio

Meshuggah is a band from Sweeden known for their heavy sound and odd time signatures. They formed in 1987 in Umeå and raised in popularity until the mid 90s when they became known world wide. Today, Meshuggah is seen as one of the most influental and talented bands in metal. Their song bleed is notorious for its complex polymeter and general difficulty to play.
